How it works

Short process. Minimal hassle. No agency jargon.

1

Send informasjon

Send oss navn på bedriften, tjenester, kontaktinfo og eventuelle bilder.

Send the basics

Send the business name, services, contact details, and any photos you have.

2

We build the page

We create a simple, mobile-friendly page with a clear structure.

Du ser gjennom og ber om små endringer før publisering.

You review it

You review the draft and request any small changes before launch.

4

Launch and follow-up

Once it looks right, we publish and can continue supporting you if needed.
